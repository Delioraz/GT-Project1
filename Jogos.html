Gran Turismo is a racing video game series known for its precision and realism in simulating cars and races. Developed by Polyphony Digital and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment, the game offers a vast selection of licensed vehicles and a variety of tracks worldwide. Players can compete in career modes, online and offline multiplayer, while enjoying impressive graphics and realistic driving physics. The series has received critical acclaim and is beloved by racing fans, becoming one of the most iconic and enduring franchises in video game history.
